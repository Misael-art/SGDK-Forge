Water tiles (as seen on the "fx" layer) animate every seven frames. The palette cycles animate every three frames, and the foreground's gold (line 8) and background's water (line 15) have 7 and 8 versions apiece, so they will only be in sync once every eight cycles (and have a total of 56 combinations). Two of the palette shades are also identical for both cycles, which makes for manual recolouring difficult... if it weren't for the fact I properly indexed the palettes on all of these graphics.
